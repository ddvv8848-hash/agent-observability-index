Frequently asked questions

Is Pydantic Logfire open source?

Yes. Licensed under MIT.

Can I self-host Pydantic Logfire?

Yes — Pydantic Logfire can run in your own infrastructure.

Is Pydantic Logfire OpenTelemetry-native?

Yes — Pydantic Logfire speaks OpenTelemetry (OTLP) natively, so there is no proprietary-SDK lock-in.

How much does Pydantic Logfire cost?

Freemium. Free monthly span allowance, then usage-based per-span pricing; SDK is OSS

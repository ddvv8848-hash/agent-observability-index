Frequently asked questions

Is New Relic AI Monitoring open source?

No — New Relic AI Monitoring is a proprietary/commercial product.

Can I self-host New Relic AI Monitoring?

No — New Relic AI Monitoring is cloud-only; there is no self-hosted deployment.

Is New Relic AI Monitoring OpenTelemetry-native?

Yes — New Relic AI Monitoring speaks OpenTelemetry (OTLP) natively, so there is no proprietary-SDK lock-in.

How much does New Relic AI Monitoring cost?

Freemium. Included in New Relic usage-based pricing: 100GB free/mo, then ~$0.40-0.60/GB ingest plus per-seat fees
